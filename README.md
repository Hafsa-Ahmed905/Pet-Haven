## Features

- **Pet Browsing** - View available pets with photos and descriptions
- **Adoption Services** - Comprehensive adoption guidance and support
- **Pet Profiles** - Detailed information about each adoptable pet
- **Educational Resources** - Pet care tips and training guidance
- **Vet Support** - Health checkup information for all pets
- **Testimonials** - Success stories from adopters
- **Contact Form** - Easy communication with the adoption team
- **Responsive Design** - Works seamlessly on all devices

## Sections

- **Home** - Hero section with call-to-action
- **Pets** - Browse available pets for adoption
- **Why Adopt** - Benefits and reasons to adopt
- **Services** - Adoption guidance, vet checkups, pet care education
- **Testimonials** - Adopter experiences and reviews
- **Contact** - Get in touch with Pet Haven
